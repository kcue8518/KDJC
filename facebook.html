Facebook / Whatsapp Hacks

Background

Facebook and Whatsapp are owned by the same company and both get hacked frequently. Facebook was created back in Feburary of 2004 and Whatsapp was created back in Feburary of 2009. Both Facebook and Whatsapp are popular social media websites that people use to communicate with their friends and family members. They also use it to share special memories, people have a lot of trust in these platforms. These platforms are not secure and at this point, hackers log in for fun or just to see if they have the skills to do it.

Facebook

In early 2021, a hacker managed to possess and sell over 1.5 billion of users data. The data contains users: name, email, phone number, location, gender, and user ID. The data appears to be authentic. The personal data obtained through web scraping. The data was sold for as cheap as $10. The sold data was claimed to be new from 2021.
