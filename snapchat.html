Background

Evan Spiegel, Reggie Brown, and Bobby Murphy founded Snapchat in 2011 at Stanford University. In July, 2011, it was originally released as “Picaboo”. Right afterward, Spiegel and Murphy forced Brown out of the company, and relaunched the app as Snapchat in September, 2011. User photos and videos can be posted on Snapchat, but disappear after a short period of time.
